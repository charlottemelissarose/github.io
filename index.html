About

Currently Head Visuals Producer & Bookings Editor at Vogue Australia and GQ Australia, Charlotte is a visual and casting producer with a strong creative eye. She is trusted to oversee visual integrity and production across print editorial, video, commercial projects, and major live events. Equally at home on set and leading the logistics, with a track record of delivering the highest level productions at the forefront of fashion.

With a background spanning M.A.P Ltd London, Magnum Photos and Trunk Archive Australia, Charlotte brings a rare breadth of experience across editorial, agency and archive worlds.

A member of the Condé Nast PhotoVogue Committee and a Portfolio Reviewer - Charlotte has spent considerable time seeking out image-makers with a genuinely unique point of view. She remains passionate about nurturing emerging creatives in a way that remains authentic to their artistic vision.
