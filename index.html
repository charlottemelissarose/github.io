Photo Credit: Joe Brennan

Head Visuals Producer at Vogue Australia and GQ Australia, responsible for curating and producing innovative visual content across print editorial, video production, major events and commercial projects.

A member of the Condé Nast PhotoVogue Committee and Portfolio Reviewer — Charlotte brings a rare breadth of experience across editorial, agency and archive worlds, with a background spanning M.A.P Ltd London, Magnum Photos and Trunk Archive Australia.

She is trusted to oversee visual integrity and production across print editorial, video, commercial projects, and major live events. Equally at home on set and behind the logistics, with a track record of delivering high-level productions at the forefront of fashion.

With considerable time spent seeking out image-makers with a genuinely unique point of view, she remains passionate about nurturing emerging creatives in a way that remains authentic to their artistic vision.
